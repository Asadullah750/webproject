An IT club is a group of students interested in information technology (IT) who come together to learn, collaborate, and participate in activities related to the field. IT clubs can be found in colleges, universities, and other institutions.

Workshops and seminars

These events help students learn new technologies and improve their technical skills

projects

Students work on projects together, such as software development or hardware design, to apply their knowledge to real-world problems

Skill development

Clubs provide resources like tutorials, online courses, and mentorship to help students develop their skills
